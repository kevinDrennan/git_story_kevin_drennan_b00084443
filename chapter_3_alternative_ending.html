Chapter 3

The Alternate

So this is it, the penultimate chapter. All of those agonisingly suspenseful chapters prior to this, building up to the cataclysmic event that will surely happen at the end of this chapter. Granting the reader respite from the absolute page turner that was this story.

Everything was fine. Robots turned out to be the single greatest invention to further the needs of humans. They assisted in building habitable colonies on numerous different planets. Humans became the masters of a planet inhabited by robots. The corporation google has been elected supreme leader for the last seven elections.
